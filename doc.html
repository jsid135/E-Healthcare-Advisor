Clinical Pathways

Find the Right Specialist

This guide helps you map common symptoms to the most relevant medical specialty.

General Medicine

  • Fever, cough, headache, general checkups
  • Good first stop for unclear symptoms

Cardiology

  • Chest pain, palpitations, breathlessness
  • Heart and blood vessel concerns

Gastroenterology

  • Abdominal pain, nausea, bowel issues
  • Digestive tract and liver concerns

Pediatrics

  • Child fever, growth, vaccination guidance
  • Care for infants and adolescents

Orthopedics

  • Joint pain, fractures, back pain
  • Muscle, bone, and ligament conditions

Neurology / Dermatology

  • Neurology: migraine, dizziness, numbness
  • Dermatology: acne, rashes, eczema

Note: This content is informational, not a substitute for medical diagnosis. For urgent symptoms, seek immediate medical care.
